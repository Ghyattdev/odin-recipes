Perfect Pizza

This will be a recipe for a perfect pizza yum!

Please make sure to follow this recipes exact instructions in order

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Red Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Salami

Instructions for Cooking

  1. Prepare Oven to 400 Degrees.
  2. Grab Dough and roll it out into circle
  3. Spread Red sauce all over pizza
  4. Add Cheese on top of Sauce
  5. Add Pepperoni and Salami to the top of the pizza
  6. Put in oven bake for 20 minutes
