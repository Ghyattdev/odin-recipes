Tacos at home

This will be a recipe to show how you make tacos at home.

Please make sure to follow this recipes exact instructions in order

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Grab Taco Shells and make sure to not break them
  2. Add cooked meat to shell
  3. Add Cheese on top of Meat
  4. Serve and Enjoy!
